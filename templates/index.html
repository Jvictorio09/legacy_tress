The story behind LegacyTress.

Founded by Christelle Bolden, LegacyTress was built on a lifelong passion for braiding and an unwavering commitment to healthy hair. What began as a love for the art has grown into one of South Florida's trusted destinations for premium protective styles.

More than beautiful hairstyles, we focus on preserving the health and strength of your natural hair while delivering looks that reflect your individuality. LegacyTress was proudly featured by VoyageMIA, where Christelle shared the journey from styling hair at home to building a luxury space for protective styling, healthy-hair care and community.
