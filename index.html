06Where Tradition Meets Modern Luxury

The story behind LegacyTress.

Founded by Christelle Bolden, LegacyTress was built on a lifelong passion for braiding and an unwavering commitment to healthy hair. What began as a love for the art of braiding has grown into one of South Florida's trusted destinations for premium protective styles.

More than creating beautiful hairstyles, we focus on preserving the health and strength of your natural hair while delivering looks that reflect your individuality. LegacyTress was proudly featured by VoyageMIA, where Christelle shared the journey from styling hair at home to building a luxury space dedicated to protective styling, healthy hair care and community.
